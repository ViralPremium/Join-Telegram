What is Adsterra?

Adsterra is a global online advertising network that helps website owners and publishers earn money by displaying ads. Advertisers promote their apps, websites, and CPA offers using the Adsterra platform.

Who can use Adsterra?

  • Website owners
  • Blog owners
  • Landing page creators
  • Telegram traffic users
  • App developers

How Adsterra works

  1. Sign up as a publisher
  2. Add your website or use a direct SmartLink
  3. Get your ad code or link
  4. Visitors view ads or offers
  5. You earn money

Types of ads in Adsterra

  • Popunder ads
  • Native banner ads
  • Display banner ads
  • Social bar ads
  • SmartLink (direct link)

What is SmartLink?

  • Automatically shows high-paying ads
  • Works without a website
  • Perfect for Telegram and WhatsApp traffic

Approval process

  • Fast and easy approval
  • No strict rules like Google AdSense
  • Works for new websites

Advantages

  • Fast payments
